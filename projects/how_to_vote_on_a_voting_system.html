How to Vote on a Voting System

It's happened to all of us—you're setting up a new country, and get to the stage of deciding on your voting system, only to find yourself in a bit of a pickle. Having heard that there are serious flaws with a number of voting systems, you want to take this rare oppurtunity to make sure your new country's system is the best one. But, being as incredibly democratic as you are, your definition of the best system is ultimately tied up in the consensus of your citizens. Simple—you will put it to a vote. But wait, before we have decided on which voting system to use, how can we have a vote? Which system should we use in this inaugural vote?
