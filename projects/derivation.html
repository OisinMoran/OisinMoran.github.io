Derivation

About

This is a mockup of a tool with the ambitious intention of becoming the standard format for derivations. The tool aims to do several things:

  • Chiefly, to become an environment to sanely create, share, and understand important derivations.
  • Propagate and make visible the assumptions made at every step in a derivation, thereby clarifying limitations, approximations, and possible errors.
  • Give a fully expandable/collapsible explanation for every step—utilising prerequisite knowledge and/or skills to compress the derivation (e.g. if the chain rule is known, a step could read "take the derivative", if it is not there could be an embedded explanation).
  • (Stretch goal: To become a new medium of thought—Smart Paper—combining character recognition, symbolic computation, spaced repetition (so you never forget how to do the steps you have collapsed), and anything else with the ability to increase your efficacy as a thinker. Whether it be in the form of increasing creativity or ensuring much fewer mistakes in your reasoning.)

Example (2=1)

Let $$a = b$$ Multiply both sides by $a$ $$a^2 = ab$$ Subtract $b^2$ from both sides $$a^2-b^2 = ab-b^2$$ Factorize (difference of squares and common term) $$(a+b)(a-b) = b(a-b)$$
Divide both sides by $(a-b)$ [only valid if $a-b \ne 0$, i.e. $a \ne b$] $$a+b = b$$ Substitution $(a=b)$ $$2b = b$$
Divide both sides by $b$ [only valid if $b \ne 0$] $$2 = 1$$
