Paperclip Game Strategic Modelling

Rand - Pick randomly A100 - Always choose A B100 - Always choose B Greedy - Choose the option with the largest potential payoff Generous - Choose the option that gives your opponent the largest potential payoff MiniMax - Choose the option that gives your opponent the smallest potential payoff Tit for Tat - Choose the option your opponent chose last round Beat Last - Choose the option that does the best against what your opponent chose last round
